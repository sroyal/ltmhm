About the Author

World traveled author and graduate of Georgia State University she earned her degree in Social Work with plans in the near future of completing her MSW. This author embarked on a new career and returned to school at age 55 graduating with honors one month after her 59th birthday. Being extremely passionate about the well being and education of all children, she created "Angela's Foundation of Hope" The mission is to empower young girls through education. The foundation would be awarding scholarships to assist teenage girls who possess the academic ability, drive, and determination to attend university but are financially unable to do so.
